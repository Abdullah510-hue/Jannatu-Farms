About Us

who we are

Jannatu Farms Organization is a non-political, Non-Governmental community agricultural development organization geared towards developing our Sierra Leonean communities through agricultural activities, including crop growing, Livestock rearing, and processing and marketing of farm produce.

Mission

At Jannatu Farms, our mission is to drive sustainable growth and economic resilience by generating stable income streams, combating the effects of inflation through innovative and resilient products, fostering an equitable environment that promotes fairness and inclusivity, and actively contributing to economic development. We are dedicated to creating lasting value for our stakeholders, empowering communities, and building a thriving, sustainable future for all.

Vision

Jannatu Farms envisions a vibrant Muslim community that embodies the values of faith and virtuous conduct, in accordance with the Quranic principle found in Surah Al-Kahf (18:107). Our vision is to establish an environment where sincere belief in Allah and the practice of righteous deeds converge, fostering individual prosperity in this world and culminating in the highest echelons of paradise, Al-Firdaus. Through spiritual nourishment via prayer, Quranic contemplation, and ethical behavior rooted in compassion and integrity, we aim to shape individuals who positively influence society. By promoting education, communal support, social justice, and the preservation of family values, we strive to cultivate holistic contributors who strike a harmonious equilibrium between worldly achievements and everlasting success. "Verily, those who believe and do righteous deeds, shall have the gardens of Al-Firdaus for their entertainment." (Quran, 18:107)

Motto

Plant with Iman and Reap with Shukr

WHAT WE DO

At Jannatu Farms, we are committed to transforming agriculture through innovation, sustainability, and community-focused initiatives. From cultivating expansive cassava and rice farms to processing quality garri and planning for large-scale rice production, we work tirelessly to ensure food security, generate economic opportunities, and promote sustainable farming practices that benefit both our community and the environment.

Key achievements

At Jannatu Farms, we take pride in the significant milestones we have achieved, reflecting our commitment to excellence, innovation, and community impact:

Cassava Farming and Processing

Successfully cultivated and managed a 60-acre cassava farm, now processing the produce into garri. To date, we have produced over 100 bags of garri, contributing to local food supply and economic activity.

Rice Farming Expansion

Established a 50-acre rice farm dedicated to planting seedlings as part of our strategic plan to expand to over 1,500 acres in the 2025 rice planting season, aiming to enhance food security and create more employment opportunities.
